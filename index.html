Hianime Video API

Official embedding documentation for MegaPlay Buzz

Overview

Use our player to embed videos easily in just one step. Our hosted content remains accessible even if the original sources change or go offline.

Endpoint Structure

https://megaplay.buzz/stream/s-2/{hianime-ep-id}/{language}

* Replace {hianime-ep-id} with the ID from hianime URL and {language} with sub or dub.
